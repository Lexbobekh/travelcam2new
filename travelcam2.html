How TravelCam Works.

Everything you need to know before your trip.

✈️

Airport pickup

Our cameras are available at Incheon and Gimhae (Busan) airports. Head to the TravelCam kiosk in arrivals — camera is charged and ready to shoot.

🏙️

City store pickup

Pick up from partner stores in Hongdae, Myeongdong (Seoul) or Haeundae (Busan). Walk-in or pre-book. Staff help you get comfortable with your camera.

📦

Drop-off anywhere

Return at any TravelCam kiosk or city drop-off. Pop it in the return box before your flight. Deposit refunded within 24 hours.

🛡️

Optional insurance

Add damage protection for +$5/day — covers drops, spills, and theft. No deductibles, no hidden fees. Just peace of mind.

💳

The buy-out option

Fallen in love with your rental? Every camera has a preset buy-out price shown clearly before you book. If you don't return it, the buy-out price is charged automatically. Think of it as a no-pressure trial before purchasing.
